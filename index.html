Bajaj Pulsar NS200 is a street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,42,051 in India.
It is available in only 1 variant and 4 colours. The Bajaj Pulsar NS200 is powered by 199.5cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 24.13 bhp and a torque of 18.74 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Bajaj Pulsar NS200 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Pulsar NS200 bike weighs 159.5 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12 liters.

The Bajaj Pulsar NS200 is positioned above the Pulsar 220F. The naked motorcycle gets three new dual-tone paint schemes- Burnt Red, Pewter Grey, Satin Blue, and Metallic Pearl White paint schemes to set it apart from the older model.
